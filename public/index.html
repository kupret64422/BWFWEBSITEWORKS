About BWF

The international governing body for badminton worldwide

About

The Badminton World Federation (BWF) is the international governing body responsible for leading and shaping the future of badminton across the globe. Established to unify and elevate the sport, the BWF oversees world-class tournaments, supports the growth of national badminton programs, and ensures the integrity and fairness of competition at every level. Through its work with players, coaches, officials, and member associations, the organization continuously strives to create opportunities for athletes to excel and for fans to experience the excitement of badminton like never before.
